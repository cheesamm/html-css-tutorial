Pancakes

Ingredients

  • Flour
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Sugar
  • Baking powder
  • Vanilla extract (optional)
  • Salt
  • Maple syrup or honey
  • Strawberries (optional)
  • Blueberries (optional)

Instructions

  1. In a bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  2. In another bowl, whisk eggs, milk, and vanilla extract
  3. Combine the wet and dry ingredients, mixing until smooth
  4. Heat a pan over medium heat and melt some butter
  5. Pour batter onto the pan to form pancakes
  6. Cook until bubbles appear on the surface, then flip
  7. Cook until golden brown on both sides
  8. Serve with syrup, fruits, or toppings of choice

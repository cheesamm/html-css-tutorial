Hamburger

Ingredients

  • Burger buns
  • Beef or chicken patty
  • Cheese slice
  • Lettuce
  • Tomato slices
  • Bacon (optional)
  • Mayonnaise or preferred sauce
  • Butter (for toasting buns)

Instructions

  1. Cook the patty on a grill or pan until fully cooked
  2. Toast the burger buns with butter until golden brown
  3. Place a slice of cheese on the hot patty to melt
  4. Layer lettuce on the bottom bun
  5. Add tomato slices on top of the lettuce
  6. Place the cooked patty with melted cheese
  7. Add crispy bacon if using
  8. Spread sauce on the top bun and assemble the burger
  9. Serve and enjoy!

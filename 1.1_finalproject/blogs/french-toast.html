French Toast

Ingredients

  • Bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Vanilla extract
  • Cinnamon (optional)
  • Sugar (optional)
  • Maple syrup or honey
  • Strawberries (optional)
  • Blueberries (optional)

Instructions

  1. Crack eggs into a bowl
  2. Add milk, cinnamon, and vanilla extract
  3. Whisk the mixture until well combined
  4. Dip bread slices into the mixture, coating both sides
  5. Heat butter in a pan over medium heat
  6. Cook the bread slices until golden brown on both sides
  7. Serve with syrup, fruits, or toppings of choice
